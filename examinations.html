Examinations

Examinations

  1. There are three major examinations in an year, i.e., First Term,Second Term and Final be sides regular Unit tests.The passmark is 40%.
  2. Promotions are given on the basis of one year’s work and not merely marks scored in the final examination. Every student must be present for all the three major exams. The average of this is taken at the end of the year.The total of 40% in each subject is a precondition for promotion.Class works and home works are also assessed and awarded.
  3. Each student is required to be present for 90% of school days as a precondition for promotion.
  4. Grades,and not ranks,are given to the children after each examination.No grade will be given if a child has failed or was absent in any subject.Answer Scripts of 1st Term and 2nd Term Examinations will be shown to the guardians.Final examination Answer Scripts will not be shown.
  5. There shall be neither re-test nor re-exam.
  6. The result declared at the end of the year is final and cannot be reconsidered. In all questions of promotion or failure, the Principal’s decision is final.
  7. Anyone who resorts to unfair means in the examination will be dismissed instantly.
  8. If a pupil does not pass in all the subjects any concession he/she enjoys will be cancelled.
